Research Interests


Local adaptation and structural variation in Zostera marina

My dissertation research is focused on how a large chromosomal inversion impacts the processes of local adaptation and parallel evolution in California eelgrass.


Urban adaptation in Battus philenor

In a collaborative project supported by the UC Davis Center for Population Biology, I am studying how the cosmopolitan butterfly Pipevine Swallowtail adapts to urban environments across its California range.


Biogeography and Taxonomy of Octocorals

I worked in the lab of Cathy McFadden at Harvey Mudd College during my undergraduate and as a laboratory technician. I applied genetic and genomic tools to map the biogeography of octocorals and understand the taxonmy of this underdescribed clade.
