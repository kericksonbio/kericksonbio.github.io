About Me


I am a PhD Candidate in the Population Biology Graduate Group at UC Davis and a member of the Bay . I am interested in how species and populations adapt to our rapidly changing world. I combine field work and experiments with modern sequencing techniques and bioinformatic tools to uncover the genetic basis of adaptation. I enjoy working in non-model plant and invertebrate systems and have a passion for marine science and conservation.

Outside of my research, you can find me swimming, biking, running, baking or petting dogs.
